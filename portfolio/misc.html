1. What is the story or inspiration behind your work of art?

My artwork was inspired by the idea of space and how it mirrors the endless reach of
imagination and emotion. I wanted to Invision that feeling of floating between thoughts, like
drifting through an endless colorful void of space. Each glowing orb represents a different
emotion or idea, suspended in a dark, limitless background.

I chose Microsoft Paint as my medium because of its ease of use and simplicity, which
allowed me to show more expression. Painting’s basic tools made me more intentional with
how I used color and placement, allowing me to be more creative with each stroke.
